I'm Zach

I'm a pretty friendly guy. I like playing music, recreating outdoors, and meeting other friendly people. I know a good deal about rocks, and I enjoy thinking about them when I am hiking. However, I don't want to think about them for a living. That's too much rocks. It is a personal aspiration of mine to one day put together a recording studio. I think food, people, mountains, and music are probably all I need to be happy.

I Like Code

After college I discovered that I'd really like to be a software engineer. It feels very nice to build things and have them work. Keeps me off the streets. I never have to think about the same stratigraphic column for six months at a time.... My three main areas of interest are discrete-time signal processing, web development, and GIS (there, I still get to look at rocks). You can check out some of my active projects below.
